sort1 uses: bubble sort

How do you know?: It's fast on sorted numbers ( omega n ) and slow on random numbers ( O(n^2) )

sort2 uses: merge sort

How do you know?: It's rather fast on sorted numbers ( omega(n log n)) and fast on random numbers ( O(n log n) )

sort3 uses: selection sort

How do you know?: It's slow on sorted numbers ( omega (n^2) ) and slow on random numbers ( O(n^2) )
